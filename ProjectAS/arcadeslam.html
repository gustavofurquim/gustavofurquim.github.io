the project

arcadeslam was a 1-year project fully developed by me (gustavo) including coding, lighting, cameras, assets, physics, etc. the game was developed in c# using unity engine. in the gif on the side we can see the stages of production of arcadeslam, starting with the sketch, and then the stages of the visual development of the game, until we reach its final product form.

the goal in arcadeslam is to score as much as you can within 30 seconds and reach the mini-stages within the game, in which the lighting and music change. the game was developed in order to improve my skills in c# and unity engine. hope you enjoy it :)
